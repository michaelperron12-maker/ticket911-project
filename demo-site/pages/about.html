Our Story

From a Montreal Startup to Three Jurisdictions

Founded in 2011 by Avi Levy

Ticket911 was born out of a simple frustration: too many drivers were paying tickets they could have contested and won. Avi Levy, with over 25 years of experience in criminal, penal, and civil law, saw firsthand how the system was stacked against everyday drivers.

Starting from a small office in Montreal, Avi built a team of like-minded lawyers — many of them former prosecutors who understood exactly how the other side operated. The idea was radical at the time: make traffic ticket defense accessible, affordable, and effective for everyone.

As word spread and the case count grew into the tens of thousands, the demand for Ticket911's services expanded beyond Quebec. The firm opened operations in Ontario, setting up an office in Whitby to serve the Greater Toronto Area and beyond. Not long after, Ticket911 crossed the border into New York, bringing the same proven defense strategies to drivers in all five boroughs.

Today, Ticket911 is the largest AI-powered traffic ticket defense platform in North America, combining the expertise of former prosecutors with cutting-edge artificial intelligence to deliver unparalleled results for clients across three jurisdictions.
