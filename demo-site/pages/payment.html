FAQ

Payment Questions

Is my payment information secure?
Absolutely. All payments are processed through PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant payment processors (Stripe and Moneris). Your card details are encrypted with 256-bit SSL and are never stored on our servers. We use the same security standards as major banks.
What currencies do you accept?
We accept Canadian Dollars (CAD) for Quebec and Ontario packages, and US Dollars (USD) for New York packages. Your bank will handle any currency conversion if needed.
Can I get a refund?
Yes. If we are unable to improve the outcome of your ticket (compared to the original fine and demerit points), you are entitled to a full refund minus a $100 administrative fee. This guarantee applies to all packages.
When does my defense start?
Immediately. Within 60 seconds of your payment, our AI begins analyzing your ticket. A lawyer is assigned within 24 hours, and you'll have full access to your client portal to track progress.
Do you offer receipts and invoices?
Yes. A professional PDF invoice is generated automatically for every payment. It includes all applicable taxes (TPS/TVQ for Quebec, HST for Ontario, NY Sales Tax). You can also export invoices to QuickBooks or Sage from your portal.
