FAQ

Pricing Questions & Answers

Everything you need to know about our pricing, packages, and payment process.

How do I know which package is right for my ticket?
Your package depends on two factors: the jurisdiction (Quebec, Ontario, or New York) and the number of demerit points on your ticket. You can find the demerit points listed on your ticket or use our free AI Ticket Analysis tool to scan your ticket — it will automatically identify the violation, demerit points, and recommend the right package for you in 30 seconds.
Are there any hidden fees beyond the package price?
No. The price you see is the price you pay, plus applicable taxes (TPS/TVQ in Quebec, HST in Ontario, NY Sales Tax in New York). There are no hourly fees, no surprise charges, and no additional court costs. Everything — AI analysis, lawyer fees, court appearances, and case management — is included in your package price.
How does the money-back guarantee work exactly?
If we cannot improve the outcome of your ticket in any way — meaning no dismissal, no fine reduction, and no demerit point reduction — you are entitled to a full refund of your package price minus a $100 administrative fee. The admin fee covers court filing costs, document preparation, and AI resources used on your case. Refunds are processed within 30 business days of the final court decision.
Can I pay in installments?
Yes. We offer a payment plan option that lets you split your package price into 2 or 3 equal installments at 0% interest and with no credit check required. The first payment is due at the time of booking, and subsequent payments are scheduled at 30-day intervals. Payment plans are available for all packages $349 and above.
What is the difference between Ontario Package 5 and Package 7?
Package 5 covers tickets with 3-4 demerit points and includes standard legal representation and court appearance. Package 7 is for more serious offenses with 5 or more demerit points, and it includes full trial representation — meaning our lawyer will represent you through the entire trial process, including cross-examination of witnesses, submission of evidence, and full legal argument. This is essential for serious charges like stunt driving or excessive speeding.
Do I need to appear in court myself?
No. In the vast majority of cases, our lawyer appears in court on your behalf and you do not need to be present. This is one of the key benefits of hiring Ticket911 — we handle everything so you can continue with your daily life. In rare situations where your presence is required (such as certain trial proceedings), we will notify you well in advance and prepare you thoroughly.
